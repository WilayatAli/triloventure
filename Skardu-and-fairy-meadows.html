8 Days Adventure: Explore Skardu & Fairy Meadows

Description

Embark on an exhilarating 8-day adventure through the stunning landscapes of Skardu and Fairy Meadows. This journey takes you from the serene beauty of Skardu to the breathtaking vistas of Fairy Meadows, offering a perfect blend of natural wonders, scenic treks, and cultural experiences. Discover the majesty of towering peaks, serene lakes, and lush meadows while immersing yourself in the vibrant local culture and hospitality.

HightLights

  • Explore the historic Skardu Fort and the serene Sheosar Lake.

  • Trek to the enchanting Fairy Meadows with stunning views of Nanga Parbat.

  • Visit the beautiful Shangrila Resort (Lower Kachura Lake) and Upper Kachura Lake.

  • Experience the local culture and hospitality of Skardu and the surrounding villages.

  • Enjoy breathtaking views of the majestic Karakoram mountain range.

  • Take a scenic drive along the Karakoram Highway and explore its picturesque landscapes.

Travel Plans

1
Day 1: Arrival in Skardu

Arrive in Skardu and transfer to your hotel.

Rest and acclimate to the altitude.

Evening at leisure or explore the local market.

2
Day 2: Explore Skardu

Visit Skardu Fort and enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding valley.

Explore Lower Kachura Lake (Shangrila Resort) and Upper Kachura Lake.

Return to your hotel and relax.

3
Day 3: Drive to Fairy Meadows

Drive from Skardu to Raikot Bridge (approximately 2-3 hours).

Cross the Indus River by jeep and commence your trek to Fairy Meadows (around 3-4 hours).

Arrive at Fairy Meadows and check into your campsite or lodge.

4
Day 4: Explore Fairy Meadows

Enjoy a full day exploring Fairy Meadows, with stunning views of Nanga Parbat, the world's ninth-highest peak.

Optional short hikes or simply relax and take in the majestic scenery.

5
Day 5: Trek to Nanga Parbat Base Camp (Optional)

Embark on a trek to Nanga Parbat Base Camp for a closer view of the towering peak (optional and depends on weather and fitness level).

Return to Fairy Meadows for the night.

6
Day 6: Return to Skardu

Trek back to Raikot Bridge and drive to Skardu.

Check into your hotel and relax after the journey.

7
Day 7: Explore Skardu and Surroundings

Visit any missed attractions or explore local markets for souvenirs.

Enjoy a relaxing day exploring the local culture or take a short excursion to nearby spots.

8
Day 8: Departure

Drive back to your departure point or continue your onward journey.

Package Includes

  • Accommodation in hotels as per the itinerary.

  • Transportation throughout the tour, including private vehicle and driver.

  • Meals as specified in the itinerary (generally breakfast).

  • Entrance fees to historical sites and attractions.

  • Local guide for cultural insights and assistance.

Package Exclude

  • International and domestic flights.

  • Meals not mentioned in the itinerary.

  • Personal expenses (e.g., souvenirs, tips).

  • Travel insurance.

  • Any additional activities not specified in the itinerary.
