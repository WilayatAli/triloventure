6-Day Gilgit, Hunza & Nagar Tour: Discover Northern Pakistan’s Gems

Description

Explore the wonders of Skardu, one of Pakistan’s most awe-inspiring destinations, on this 6-day adventure. From the scenic landscapes of Naran Kaghan or Chilas on your way to Skardu to the majestic lakes, deserts, and fortresses of Baltistan, this tour offers the perfect mix of natural beauty, history, and adventure.

HightLights

  • Skardu Valley: Known for its rugged beauty, clear blue lakes, towering mountains, and rich culture.

  • Shangrila Resort (Lower Kachura Lake): Often called "Heaven on Earth," this scenic lake is a must-see.

  • Sheosar Lake: One of the highest lakes in the world, nestled in Deosai National Park.

  • Deosai Plains: Known as the "Land of Giants," this high-altitude plateau is a stunning expanse of wilderness.

  • Shigar Fort & Khaplu Fort: Explore historical fortresses that offer a glimpse into the region’s rich heritage.

  • Cold Desert: Witness the world's highest cold desert with its incredible sand dunes.

Travel Plans

1
Day 1: Islamabad to Naran/Kaghan or Chilas

The journey begins with an early morning departure from Islamabad, heading north along the scenic Karakoram Highway. Depending on the season, the trip will take 8 to 10 hours, with a breakfast stop en route at either Mansehra or Balakot. Travelers will then have the option to stop for lunch and sightseeing in Naran or Kaghan, or continue to Chilas if the Babusar Pass is closed. In the evening, the group will arrive in either Naran/Kaghan or Chilas, where they will check into a hotel for a comfortable overnight stay, ready to enjoy the beauty of the region.

2
Day 2: Naran/Kaghan or Chilas to Skardu

After breakfast, the journey continues towards Skardu, spanning 8 to 10 hours and showcasing dramatic landscapes along the way, including stunning views of the Indus River. A lunch stop will be made at a local restaurant in Jaglot or near the confluence of the Indus and Gilgit rivers. Upon arrival in Skardu, guests will check into their hotel and have the evening to relax and unwind, soaking in the beauty of the area. An overnight stay in Skardu allows for a peaceful night in this enchanting region.

3
Day 3: Explore Skardu and Shangrila Resort

After breakfast, the morning begins with a visit to the famous Shangrila Resort, also known as "Heaven on Earth," where guests can marvel at the stunning Lower Kachura Lake surrounded by lush greenery. Lunch will be enjoyed at the resort, allowing everyone to take in the serene atmosphere. In the afternoon, the group will explore Upper Kachura Lake, a less crowded but equally breathtaking spot renowned for its clear blue waters. As evening approaches, everyone will return to Skardu for dinner and some free time to relax and enjoy the evening. An overnight stay in Skardu offers a chance to unwind in this picturesque setting.

4
Day 4: Deosai Plains and Sheosar Lake

After an early breakfast, the group embarks on a full-day excursion to Deosai National Park, one of the highest plateaus in the world, located just 2-3 hours away. The first stop is Sheosar Lake, renowned for its breathtaking views and surrounded by majestic snow-capped peaks. A packed lunch or picnic will be enjoyed in the serene setting of Deosai. In the afternoon, the adventure continues as guests explore the vast beauty of the Deosai Plains, with opportunities to spot wildlife such as Himalayan brown bears and golden marmots. As evening approaches, the group will return to Skardu for dinner, followed by a restful overnight stay, reflecting on a day filled with natural wonders.

5
Day 5: Shigar Fort, and Cold Desert

After breakfast, the morning begins with a scenic drive to Shigar Valley, where the group will visit the magnificent Shigar Fort, a beautifully restored heritage site that provides insights into the region's rich history. Lunch will be enjoyed at the fort or at a nearby local restaurant. In the afternoon, the adventure continues with a visit to the Cold Desert, where guests can marvel at incredible sand dunes set against a backdrop of snow-covered peaks. As evening falls, there will be free time for shopping in the Skardu bazaar or simply relaxing. An overnight stay in Skardu allows everyone to unwind after a day of exploration.

6
Day 6: Skardu to Islamabad via Naran/Kaghan or Chilas

After breakfast, the return journey to Islamabad begins, retracing the scenic path through the mountains. A lunch stop will be made en route in either Naran or Kaghan, or in Chilas depending on the season. The afternoon will involve continued driving back, with opportunities for scenic stops to capture the breathtaking landscapes along the way. As evening approaches, the group will arrive in Islamabad, marking the end of the tour and reflecting on the unforgettable experiences shared throughout the journey.
