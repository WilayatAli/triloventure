8 Days Spring Serenity: Explore the Blossoming Beauty of Hunza and Skardu

Description

Spring brings an extraordinary charm to Hunza and Skardu, transforming them into a mesmerizing paradise. As the season unfolds, vibrant blossoms of cherry, apricot, apple, pear, and peach adorn the landscape, set against a backdrop of snow-capped mountains. This stunning display of nature’s beauty offers awe-inspiring contrasts that captivate the senses.
Beyond the breathtaking scenery, immerse yourself in the local culture with traditional folk music, adding an extra touch of joy to your experience. The optimal time to explore this springtime wonder is from mid-March to the end of April. Join us to witness the season’s splendor and create lasting memories.

HightLights

  • Enchanting Lakes: Visit stunning lakes like Attabad and Batura.

  • Majestic Glaciers: Explore glaciers such as Passu, Batura, and Hussaini.

  • Historic Forts: Discover the rich history with visits to Altit and Baltit forts.

  • Iconic Routes: Traverse the Karakoram Highway and the ancient Silk Route

  • River and Forts: Experience the grandeur of the Indus River, Shigar Fort, Khaplu Fort, and Kharpocho Fort.

  • Unique Desert: Explore the unique Sarfaranga Cold Desert, a testament to the region's diverse landscapes

Travel Plans

1
Pick up From Islamabd / rawalpindi

Embark on a remarkable 11-12 hour drive to Chilas, covering 461 km along the legendary Karakoram Highway. This historic route, linking the Gilgit-Baltistan region with the ancient Silk Road, spans approximately 1,300 km (810 mi) from Kashgar in China's Xinjiang region to Abbottabad and Hassan Abdal in Pakistan. As you traverse this awe-inspiring highway, you'll witness some of the world's most stunning landscapes, including towering peaks, deep valleys, and rugged terrains, all while experiencing the rich history and unique cultural tapestry of this extraordinary corridor.

2
Drive from Chilas to Skardu

On Day 2, embark on an enchanting drive from Chilas to Skardu, a journey of approximately 290 km that typically takes 8-10 hours. This scenic route meanders through dramatic mountain landscapes and alongside the Indus River, offering breathtaking views of the Karakoram range. As you travel, you'll pass through picturesque villages and experience the region's rugged beauty. Enjoy a mid-day stop for lunch at a local spot before arriving in Skardu by late afternoon or early evening. Check into your accommodation, savor a delightful dinner featuring traditional Balti cuisine, and unwind in preparation for the adventures that lie ahead.

3
Local visits in Skardu & Shigar

Morning drive to Shigar valley 35 km, visit Shigar Fort, Shigar valley and the Cold Desert, also known as the Katpana Desert. Afternoon visit Satpara Lake and hike to Kharpocho fort from Skardu city 2 hours.

4
Excursion to Khaplu Village

Drive 100 km to Khaplu village, where you'll explore the historic Chaqchan Mosque, the imposing Khaplu Fort, and the charming village itself. In the evening, return to Skardu with a 100 km drive back.

5
Drive from Skardu to Gilgit & Hunza

Drive 240 km to Gilgit and enjoy lunch upon arrival. Afterward, explore Gilgit Bazaar and visit the Kargah Buddha. Continue your journey to Hunza Valley with a 100 km drive, making stops at scenic viewpoints along the way for photography and refreshments.

6
Karimabad- Visit Baltit Fort and Altit Fort

In the morning, explore the 700-year-old Baltit Fort and the surrounding Baltit village. After lunch, visit the 900-year-old Altit Fort and the nearby Altit village, including a stop at the women carpenters' project. In the evening, enjoy a scenic walk along the water channel from Karimabad towards Hyderabad village.

7
Visit Hopper and Duiker valley

In the morning, embark on an excursion to Hopper Valley, where you can visit the impressive Hopper Glacier and explore the valley's stunning landscapes. In the afternoon, drive for approximately thirty minutes to Duiker Valley and check into your hotel. Duiker Valley offers breathtaking viewpoints for watching both the sunrise and sunset over the majestic mountains surrounding Hunza.

8
Duiker- Gulmit- Passu & Sost

Drive 50 km to Passu, making several noteworthy stops along the way. Visit the stunning Attabad Lake, followed by Borith Lake. Explore Gulkin and Gulmit villages before admiring the Passu White Glacier and Passu village. Continue to Hussani village to see the Hussani Suspension Bridge. Conclude your journey with a 50 km drive to Sost.

9
Khunjerab Pass and Karimabad

Take an excursion to Khunjerab Pass, the border between Pakistan and China. In the evening, drive back to Karimabad, enjoying the scenic return journey.

10
Drive from Karimabad to Besham

Drive 235 km to Chilas, then continue your journey after lunch along the Karakoram Highway for another 200 km to Besham. Upon arrival, transfer to your hotel for the night.

11
Drive from Besham to Islamabad
