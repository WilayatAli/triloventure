4-Day Kashmir Tour: Explore the Beauty and Culture

Description

Immerse yourself in the pristine beauty of Kashmir with Trailo Venture's 4-day tour. Discover the lush green valleys, crystal-clear rivers, and majestic mountains of Azad Kashmir. This tour combines natural beauty, cultural exploration, and relaxation, offering an ideal escape for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

HightLights

  • Muzaffarabad: The capital of Azad Jammu & Kashmir, rich in history and scenic spots.

  • Neelum Valley: A breathtaking valley with meadows, streams, and picturesque landscapes.

  • Keran & Sharda: Famous for their riverside views, lush greenery, and serene atmosphere.

  • Arrang Kel: A stunning village located at a high altitude, surrounded by mountains and forests.

Travel Plans

1
Day 1: Islamabad to Muzaffarabad (Neelum Valley)

The journey begins with an early morning departure from Islamabad to Mingora, covering a distance of 247 km which typically takes about 5 to 6 hours via the Swat Motorway. On the way, you’ll stop for breakfast in Mardan or Swabi. Upon arrival in Mingora, you can check into your hotel and freshen up before starting your afternoon exploration. Spend your time discovering Mingora city, visiting local markets, and exploring the Swat Museum to delve into the rich Buddhist heritage of the valley. In the evening, unwind with a relaxing walk along the Swat River at Fizagat Park. For dinner, you can choose to dine at a local restaurant or enjoy a meal at the hotel. The night will be spent in Mingora.

2
Day 2: Keran to Sharda Exploration

After breakfast on the second day, depart for Sharda, a picturesque village located 60 km away, which takes about 2 hours to reach. In Sharda, explore the ancient ruins of Sharda University, hike through the beautiful meadows, and take in the scenic views of the Neelum River. For lunch, you can either dine at a local restaurant in Sharda or enjoy a picnic by the river. The afternoon offers free time to further explore the surrounding areas or embark on a short trek. In the evening, return to Keran for dinner and an overnight stay.

3
Day 3: Sharda to Arrang Kel

After breakfast, embark on a jeep ride to Kel, followed by a hike or cable car ride up to the charming village of Arrang Kel. In Arrang Kel, explore the picturesque village, which is surrounded by lush forests and majestic mountains. It’s an ideal location for nature walks, photography, and soaking in the tranquil beauty of the valley. Enjoy a packed lunch or dine at a local eatery in Arrang Kel. The afternoon provides free time to relax, further explore the village, or venture on additional hikes into the valley. In the evening, return to either Keran or Sharda for dinner and an overnight stay.

3
Day 4: Return to Islamabad

After breakfast, check out and start your journey back to Islamabad. Enjoy lunch en route, either in Muzaffarabad or at another scenic stop. Continue your drive, making occasional stops to take in the beautiful scenery along the way. Arrive in Islamabad in the evening, bringing your tour to a close.
