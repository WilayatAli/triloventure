20-Days K2 Base Camp & Gondogoro La Trek: Epic High Altitude Adventures

Description

Embark on a mesmerizing 14-day journey through the rugged Karakoram Range with Broad Peak Adventures' Gondogoro La Pass Trek. This unforgettable odyssey leads you through breathtaking landscapes, from the road journey to Askole and the enchanting Braldu River to the stunning Baltoro Glacier and the panoramic marvel of Concordia, where K2 and the Gasherbrum range dominate the skyline. Experience the thrill of summiting the high-altitude Gondogoro La Pass at 5,940 meters, and enjoy a new perspective as you descend into the charming Hushe Valley. The trek concludes with a scenic journey back to Skardu and a comfortable return flight to Islamabad, leaving you with lasting memories of an extraordinary adventure.

HightLights

  • Arrival in Islamabad: Begin your adventure with a warm welcome at Islamabad International Airport, followed by a briefing and overnight stay in the city.

  • Scenic Flight to Skardu: Enjoy breathtaking aerial views of Nanga Parbat and the Himalayan peaks on your flight to Skardu, or opt for a scenic road journey if flights are unavailable.

  • Pre-Trek Exploration in Skardu: Take a day to prepare for the trek, with a final gear check and exploration of Skardu's vibrant bazaars.

  • Journey to Askole: Drive through rugged terrain to Askole, the last village before the wilderness, and start your trek with an overnight camp.

  • Trek Through the Braldu River Gorge: Experience picturesque landscapes as you trek along the Braldu River, crossing wooden bridges and setting up camp in Jhula and Paiju.

  • Concordia – The Throne Room of the Gods: Reach the heart of the Karakoram at Concordia, where you’ll be surrounded by iconic peaks including K2, Broad Peak, and the Gasherbrum range.

  • Summit Gondogoro La Pass: Challenge yourself with the ascent of Gondogoro La Pass (5,940m) and enjoy unparalleled views of the Karakoram range before descending to Khuspang.

  • Return to Civilization: After descending to Hushe, travel back through the scenic Shyok and Indus river valleys to Skardu, and enjoy the comforts of a hotel before your return flight to Islamabad.

Travel Plans

1
Day 1: Arrival in Islamabad

Arrive in Islamabad, where our team will warmly welcome you at the airport. Transfer to your hotel for a restful night and a briefing on your exciting trek ahead.

2
Day 2: Islamabad to Skardu

Take an early morning flight to Skardu or drive if flights are unavailable. Enjoy stunning views of Nanga Parbat and other peaks. Check into your Skardu hotel and relax for the day.

3
Day 3: Skardu - Pre-Trek Preparation

Use this day to finalize your trek preparations, including a gear check and last-minute shopping. Explore Skardu’s local markets and attractions at your leisure.

4
Day 4: Skardu to Askole

Embark on a 6-7 hour 4x4 drive to Askole, the last village before the wilderness. Meet your local team and begin your trek, camping in Askole for the night.

5
Day 5: Askole to Jhula

Trek through scenic landscapes along the Braldu River, crossing a wooden bridge en route to your next campsite in Jhula.

6
Day 6: Jhula to Paiju

Continue your journey along the Braldu River, passing Bardumal, and set up camp for the night in Paiju.

7
Day 7: Paiju (Acclimatization Day)

Spend the day acclimatizing in Paiju. Enjoy the stunning surroundings or take time to relax and prepare for the trek ahead.

8
Day 8: Paiju to Khuburse
Trek further into the Karakoram, setting up camp at Khuburse, where you'll be surrounded by the impressive Uli Biaho Towers.

9
Day 9: Khuburse to Urdukas

Ascend towards Urdukas, offering panoramic views of peaks like Masherbrum. Camp at Urdukas for breathtaking vistas.

10
Day 10: Urdukas to Goro II

Hike along the Baltoro Glacier to Goro II, a high-altitude camp with spectacular views of the glacier and peaks such as Gasherbrum IV.

11
Day 11: Goro II to Concordia

Traverse the Baltoro Glacier to Concordia, the heart of the Karakoram. Camp here and marvel at the stunning views of K2, Broad Peak, and Gasherbrum I and II.

12
Day 12: Concordia (Exploration Day)

Explore Concordia, known as the "Throne Room of the Gods." Optionally, visit Broad Peak Base Camp or other nearby sites.

13
Day 13: Concordia to Ali Camp

Begin your journey towards Gondogoro La Pass, setting up camp at Ali Camp amid rugged mountain scenery.

14
Day 14: Ali Camp to Gondogoro La Pass and Khuspang

Start early for the challenging ascent of Gondogoro La Pass (5,940m). Enjoy breathtaking views of the Karakoram range before descending to Khuspang for your final night in the wilderness.

15
Day 15: Khuspang to Hushe

Continue your descent to Hushe Valley. Drive to the charming village of Hushe, where you’ll camp and interact with the welcoming locals.

16
Day 16: Hushe to Skardu

Travel through the picturesque Shyok and Indus river valleys back to Skardu. Enjoy a comfortable stay in your hotel with hot showers and relaxation.

17
Day 17: Explore Skardu

Discover the beauty and culture of Skardu, exploring local attractions and enjoying the city’s unique charm

18
Day 18: Skardu to Islamabad

Fly back to Islamabad, savoring your final views of the Karakoram. Relax at your hotel or explore the city at your leisure.

19
Day 19: Departure

Depending on your flight schedule, you may have time for last-minute shopping or sightseeing. Depart from Islamabad, concluding your unforgettable Gondogoro La Pass Trek.

20
Day 20: Spare Day

A reserved day to accommodate any unforeseen delays or issues during the tour, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience.
