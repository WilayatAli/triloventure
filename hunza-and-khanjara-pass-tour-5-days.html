5-Day Hunza and Khunjerab Pass China Border Tour Local

Description

Experience the mesmerizing landscapes of Hunza Valley, combined with the thrill of visiting the highest paved border crossing in the world, Khunjerab Pass. This 5-day tour promises breathtaking mountain views, ancient forts, serene valleys, and the stunning Karakoram Highway leading to the Pakistan-China border. Perfect for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts alike!

HightLights

  • Hunza Valley: Famous for its dramatic landscapes, rich history, and welcoming locals.

  • Altit & Baltit Forts: Explore centuries-old forts with stunning views of the valley.

  • Attabad Lake: A turquoise lake formed after a landslide, offering boating and scenic views.

  • Khunjerab Pass: The highest paved international border crossing between Pakistan and China at 4,693 meters.

  • Passu Cones: Iconic pointed peaks, a must-see for photographers.

Travel Plans

1
Day 1: Islamabad to Naran/Chilas

On Day 1, you’ll embark on a journey of approximately 470 kilometers, which will take around 10 to 12 hours. Departing early from Islamabad, you'll head north along the Karakoram Highway, a route known for its breathtaking landscapes. Plan a breakfast stop en route in either Mansehra or Balakot to recharge. For lunch, you have the option to stop in Naran or Chilas, depending on the route you choose—Babusar Top is accessible in the summer months and offers stunning vistas. Upon arrival in Chilas, check into your hotel for the night. Spend the evening relaxing and taking in the picturesque views of the surrounding mountains before settling in for an overnight stay in Chilas.

2
Day 2: Chilas to Hunza Valley (Karimabad)

After breakfast, depart from Chilas and embark on the scenic 6-7 hour drive along the Karakoram Highway towards Hunza. Upon arriving in Karimabad, check into your hotel and take some time to settle in. Start your exploration with a guided tour of the historic Baltit Fort, where you'll be treated to panoramic views of the valley. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant in Karimabad, savoring regional flavors. In the afternoon, visit the UNESCO World Heritage site of Altit Fort and wander through the picturesque Altit village. As evening approaches, you have the option to explore the Karimabad bazaar or simply relax at your hotel. Spend the night in Karimabad, soaking in the serene atmosphere of this stunning region.

3
Day 3: Explore Hunza and Visit Attabad Lake

After breakfast, head out to visit the breathtaking Attabad Lake, where you can engage in optional activities such as boating or simply relax by the serene, turquoise waters. Continue your journey towards Passu, making a stop to marvel at the iconic Passu Cones with their dramatic, jagged peaks. For lunch, stop at a local spot near Gulmit or Sost to enjoy a meal while taking in the surrounding scenery. In the afternoon, explore the charming village of Passu and experience a thrilling walk across the Hussaini Suspension Bridge, offering spectacular views of the area’s natural beauty. Return to Karimabad in the evening for a relaxing dinner and overnight stay, soaking in the tranquility of the region.

4
Khunjerab Pass (China Border)

After an early breakfast, set off for Khunjerab Pass, the highest border crossing in the world, situated 120 km away and about 3-4 hours from Karimabad. As you drive along the Karakoram Highway, be sure to enjoy the stunning mountain vistas, including the majestic peaks of the Karakoram Range. Keep your eyes peeled for wildlife in Khunjerab National Park, where you might spot ibex, yaks, and even elusive snow leopards. Upon reaching Khunjerab Pass, take in the breathtaking views from this high-altitude point that connects Pakistan to China. Enjoy a packed lunch at the pass or at a nearby restaurant in Sost. In the afternoon, return to Karimabad, stopping along the way to capture more scenic views and memorable photographs. Upon arrival, you’ll have free time to relax and explore Karimabad at your leisure before an overnight stay.

5
Day 5: Hunza to Islamabad

After breakfast, check out of your hotel and start your journey back to Islamabad. On the way, stop for lunch at a scenic spot, choosing between Chilas or Naran to enjoy a meal amidst beautiful surroundings. Continue your drive to Islamabad, taking advantage of any additional scenic stops along the route to capture the last glimpses of the stunning landscapes. Arrive in Islamabad in the evening, bringing your memorable tour to a close.
