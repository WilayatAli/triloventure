3-Day Naran, Kaghan & Babusar Top: Scenic Adventure Awaits

Description

Embark on a thrilling 3-day journey through the mesmerizing landscapes of Naran, Kaghan Valley, and Babusar Top with Trailo Venture. This package is designed for local Pakistani travelers who want to experience the beauty of Pakistan's northern areas, with a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation.

HightLights

  • Naran: Nestled at an altitude of 2,409 meters (7,904 feet), this picturesque town is famous for its cool climate, lush greenery, and riverside charm.

  • Kaghan Valley: Stretching over 160 km, this stunning valley is known for its scenic beauty, glaciers, and crystal-clear lakes.

  • Saif-ul-Malook Lake: A majestic alpine lake at an elevation of 3,224 meters (10,578 feet), surrounded by snow-capped peaks. Naran: Nestled at an altitude of 2,409 meters (7,904 feet), this picturesque town is famous for its cool climate, lush greenery, and riverside charm.

  • Babusar Top: Located at an elevation of 4,173 meters (13,691 feet), this high mountain pass offers panoramic views of the surrounding valleys.

  • Lulusar Lake: A serene lake at 3,410 meters (11,190 feet) with crystal-clear waters reflecting the nearby mountains.

Travel Plans

1
Day 1: Islamabad to Naran (via Kaghan Valley)

Depart early in the morning from Islamabad for a drive of approximately 280 km, which will take about 8 hours. Stop for breakfast in Balakot, a charming town that serves as the gateway to Kaghan Valley. As you drive through Kaghan Valley, relish the panoramic views of the lush greenery, flowing streams, and majestic peaks that define this scenic route. Upon reaching Naran, check in at your hotel and then take a jeep ride to the legendary Saif-ul-Malook Lake, known for its pristine beauty and serene environment. Return to Naran in the evening for dinner and a restful overnight stay at your comfortable hotel.

2
Day 2: Naran to Babusar Top (via Lulusar Lake)

After breakfast, set out for Babusar Top, a drive of about 70 km that will take approximately 3 hours. En route, make a stop at the stunning Lulusar Lake to marvel at its serene beauty and capture some memorable photos. Upon reaching Babusar Top, take in the breathtaking views from one of Pakistan's highest points and enjoy a photo session. On your way back to Naran, stop for lunch. Once you arrive in Naran, you'll have some free time to explore the town. In the evening, gather around a bonfire for a cozy barbecue dinner, followed by an overnight stay in Naran.

3
Day 3: Naran to Islamabad

Enjoy breakfast at the hotel and spend the morning either shopping for local souvenirs or exploring Naran at your leisure. Then, begin your journey back to Islamabad, making scenic stops along the way to capture any last views of the beautiful landscapes. Arrive in Islamabad by late evening, marking the end of your memorable trip.
